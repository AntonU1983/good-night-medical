What is Sleep Apnea
Sleep apnea is a chronic sleep disorder found most commonly in men and women after menopause. Symptoms include: loud snoring, waking up gasping for air, choking or coughing and excessive daytime tiredness. If not properly treated sleep apnea can lead to high blood pressure, heart problems, stroke, Type 2 diabetes, metabolic syndrome and liver issues
